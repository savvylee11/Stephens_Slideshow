Savannah Stephens

Journalist, Web Developer, Disney Fan

Savannah Stephens is currently an Editorial Aide in the Features Section of the Washington Post. She works directly with the Classical Music, Dance, and Theatre critics and helps to organize the Arts Previews that happen every Spring, Summer, and Fall.

Before her work at The Post, she was an intern at the BBC where she wrote about viral news. She has also worked for various members of Congress and holds bachelors degrees in History and Communications. In her spare time you can find her at the Happiest Place on Earth, Walt Diseny World.
